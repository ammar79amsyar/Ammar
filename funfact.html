Luxury Fashion Funfacts

Chanel No.5 was the first perfume launched by a fashion designer.

Louis Vuitton started as a trunk maker in 1854.

Gucci double G logo represents Guccio Gucci initials.

Prada began as a leather goods shop in Milan.

Hermès still uses hand stitching for many of its products.
