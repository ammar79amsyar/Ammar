About Us

Learn more about the purpose and objectives behind De Embodies Luxury.

Website Purpose

De Embodies Luxury was created to showcase the history, founders, iconic products and achievements of the world's most prestigious luxury fashion houses. This website serves as an educational platform for fashion enthusiasts and students.

Project Objectives

  • Provide information about luxury fashion brands.
  • Highlight influential founders and designers.
  • Present iconic luxury products.
  • Explore the historical development of luxury fashion.
  • Improve web development skills through project-based learning.
