Luxury Fashion Timeline

Explore the key events that shaped the world's most prestigious fashion houses.

Historical Development

1837

Hermès Founded

Thierry Hermès established Hermès in Paris as a workshop producing harnesses and saddles.

1854

Louis Vuitton Founded

Louis Vuitton opened his first workshop specializing in luxury travel trunks.

1910

Chanel Founded

Coco Chanel opened her first boutique and transformed women's fashion.

1921

Gucci Founded

Guccio Gucci launched Gucci in Florence, Italy.

1925

Fendi Founded

Fendi was established in Rome and became known for luxury leather goods.

1946

Dior Founded

Christian Dior founded the House of Dior in Paris.

1947

Dior New Look Collection

Dior introduced the New Look collection, changing post-war fashion worldwide.

1961

Saint Laurent Founded

Yves Saint Laurent established his own luxury fashion house.

1978

Versace Founded

Gianni Versace launched Versace in Milan, Italy.

1990s - Present

Global Luxury Expansion

Luxury brands expanded into global markets and became international lifestyle companies.
